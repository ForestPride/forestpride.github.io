My Passions

I'm on a journey to explore the space where science, technology, and art combine to create beautiful, motivating, and inspiring things. My favorite fields of study are astrophysics, computer programming, and photography, but I'm eternally curious about every aspect of our universe. I publish my photography under my pseudonym, Forest de Santos. Check out my work in the Projects section and see how I'm using my skills as an amateur astronomer and all-around nerd to bring my vision to life.

Education and Experience

I have a Bachelor's of Art in astrophysics and fine art photography. However, it is my core belief that our thirst for knowledge transcends formal education. I stay curious and my interests, like our universe, are always expanding. For the past eight years, I have had the privilege of working in the technology industry where I have developed an enthusiasm for programming.
